## Electronika B3-18 mod

**DevEBox STM32F407** +
**PCM-5102** +
**TFT R61509V 400x240 3"**

### Pins:


**Pin** | **PCM-5102** 
--------- | -----------
PB15 | DIN (Data)
PB13 | BCK (Bit clock)
PB12 | LCK (Word clock)

**Pin** | **TFT**
--------- | -----------
PA0 | DB0
... | ...
PA6 | DB6 (X+)
PA7 | DB7 (Y-)
PC1 | CS
PC2 | WR (Y+)
PC3 | RS (X-)
PC4 | RD
PC5 | RST
PB8 | LED (PWM)

**Pin** | **Encoder**
--------- | -----------
PE9 | A
PE10 | Press Button
PE11 | B

**Pin** | **NUMPAD**
--------- | -----------
PE0 | ROW0
PE1 | ROW1
PE2 | ROW2
PE3 | ROW3
PE4 | COL0
PE5 | COL1
PE6 | COL2
PE7 | COL3
PE8 | COL4
